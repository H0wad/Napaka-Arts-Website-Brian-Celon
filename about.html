What is Napaka Arts?

Napaka Arts is a festival showcased at Catholic Cathedral College.
The main purpose of the festival is to highlight the Filipino culture
and the different tradition's that the Philippines has. The first ever
Napaka Arts festival was held in 2019 with the theme being school.

Founder:

Ms. Zapanta founded Napaka Arts in 2019 along with the help of fellow
Filipino students. The idea first came about as she wanted to spread the Filipino culture
to the wider community and to the students of Catholic Cathedral College. She has helped with everything from the front end to the actual
performances.
